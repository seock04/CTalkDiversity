2th Cultural Talk For Diversity

Theme: Language, Nation & Identity in Asia

April 15, 2023Moderator: Jane Kim

Event Details

Date: 15 April, 2023

Time: 07:00 (EDT) / 12:00 (BST) / 13:00 (CEST) / 18:00 (WIB) / 19:00 (PHST / CST) / 20:00 (KST) / 21:00 (AEST)

This event has already taken place and is now part of our archive.

Conference Overview

This session focused on language as a carrier of identity, belonging, and national memory. With perspectives from Indonesia, Taiwan, and the Philippines, it explored how language can unify diverse populations, reflect historical change, and help people make sense of who they are today.
