Conference Overview

This session invites participants to think about decolonization not only as a historical subject, but as a continuing question of identity, memory, and power. The conversation is designed to help the audience reflect on how inherited narratives shape present-day assumptions, relationships, and ways of seeing the world.

Pavle Luger

Experienced intercultural trainer and travel professional

Pavle Luger is an experienced intercultural trainer and travel professional with many decades of international experience working across diverse cultural contexts. His background in multilingual communication and global facilitation is complemented by his work as a certified breathwork facilitator and modern somatic practices to support clarity, resilience, and holistic well-being.

Topic: Decolonization through Recolonization

This presentation explores how the pain and influence of a colonial past often remain part of both personal identity and national history, even when people try to move on from it. Understanding how the past continues to shape the present requires awareness and adaptation, not simple rejection.
