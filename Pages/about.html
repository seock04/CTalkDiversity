About

Cultural Talk For Diversity is a supportive community where people exchange insights on culture and DEI, creating spaces where everyone helps each other to learn more, share knowledge, and grow together through meaningful projects.

Purpose

A community dedicated to broadening cultural understanding and sharing insights through the lens of DEI (Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion). By hosting monthly online sessions with experts and speakers from diverse backgrounds, we aim to uncover hidden perspectives and foster a deeper understanding of the world around us.

Mission

To cultivate a supportive and inclusive community where members share insights on culture and DEI. We strive to create a space where everyone can learn from one another, exchange knowledge, and achieve collective growth through participation in meaningful projects.

Vision

To evolve into an influential platform that connects people and ideas, serving as a catalyst for personal growth and transformative social change.
