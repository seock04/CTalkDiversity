Cultural Talk Community Organizer Team Workshop – Planning for 2026

The Cultural Talk organizer team gathered for a dedicated workshop to reflect on where the community is today and to plan intentionally for 2026. The session created space to align personal motivations with community priorities and to imagine how Cultural Talk can grow with even more clarity, care, and connection.

During the workshop, the team discussed and defined:

  • Individual needs and next actions
  • Community mission and vision
  • New event and activity ideas for 2026

Workshop Moments

Why It Matters

This workshop is an important behind-the-scenes step in strengthening the Cultural Talk community. By clarifying mission, vision, and next actions together, the organizer team is building a stronger foundation for future conferences, collaborations, and community activities.

In 2026, the team aims to broaden horizons, embrace diverse perspectives, and create more meaningful connections through a stronger lineup of events and shared community work.
