Call for Speakers: Navigating the Impact of Immigrant Policy on Identity & Society

How Do Laws Shape the Stories of Our Lives? - Cultural Talk 2026

March 8, 2026 Open Call

Theme Overview

Immigrant policy is not just about borders and documents — it is about identity, belonging, and the future of our communities. We are seeking speakers to share powerful insights and personal perspectives on how immigration policies shape cultural integration and individual journeys.

Suggested Topics include:

  • The human face behind policy
  • Inclusion vs. assimilation
  • Building a more inclusive global community
