Call for Speakers: How Culture Shapes Our Inner Worlds

"Is happiness the same everywhere?" — exploring the cultural DNA of emotions.

Theme Overview

We are looking for speakers to explore the diverse ways emotions are experienced, expressed, and understood across different cultures. This Call for Speakers invites participants to reflect on the cultural DNA of feelings and ask a deceptively simple question: is happiness really the same everywhere?

We welcome perspectives that connect culture, emotion, language, relationships, and collective norms in ways that help people understand both themselves and one another more deeply.

We are looking for insights on:

  • Emotions beyond translation
  • Cultural norms and emotional display
  • Emotions as a collective experience

Event Format

  • Each speaker delivers a 10-minute speech, followed by a 20+ minute discussion with all participants.
